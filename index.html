About Us

Shawa Advisory & Management Services (SAM Services) is a multidisciplinary Zambian firm dedicated to empowering businesses, institutions, and individuals through trusted expertise in human resources, tax advisory, immigration solutions, and evidence-based research.

We believe that people are the most powerful drivers of progress. That's why we deliver human-centered, results-oriented strategies tailored to your unique needs, leveraging deep industry insight and data-informed decision-making to drive operational excellence.

With a growing national footprint and a reputation for integrity, agility, and sustainable impact, we proudly partner with clients to unlock their potential and achieve measurable, lasting results.

Whether you're expanding your workforce, optimizing tax strategies, navigating immigration procedures, or seeking research support, our experienced team is ready to help you thrive in a complex and evolving environment.

Our services are grounded in the values of Client-Centricity, Integrity, Responsibility, Collaboration, Learning & Innovation, and Excellence — a philosophy we call CIRCLE, guiding every solution we deliver.
